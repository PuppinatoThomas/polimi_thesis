### Standardization Rules Applied

* **price($):**

  * Removed `$` and commas; kept as float (e.g., `"$774000"` → `774000`).
  * If a range existed (e.g., `"30.000 - 35.000"`), split into min/max (not present here).

* **bed / bath:**

  * Converted number words (`one`, `two`, `three`, …) to digits.
  * Left `NaN` where missing.
* **acre_lot, house_size:**

  * All converted to float (scientific notation kept as decimal).
* **brokered_by:**

  * Empty strings standardized to `-1`.
* **status:**

  * Preserved original categories (`sold`, `for_sale`, `s`, `f`, `Unknown`, `-`).
* **Dates (prev_sold_date):**

  * Standardized to `YYYY-MM-DD` where a valid date was present (e.g., `02/14/22` → `2022-02-14`).
  * Left `Unknown` or missing as-is.
